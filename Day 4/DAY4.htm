Instant Noodles Recipe

  1. Bring a desired quantity of water to a boil
  2. Add desired quantity of instant noodles
  3. Simmer for 3 minutes
  4. Add spice packet or preferred seasoning
  5. Stir and simmer for two minutes
  6. Ready to serve
